Watermelonade

When life gives you watermelons, make the Best watermelonade Ever! This aplty named recipe is as good as it gets: Sweet, tart, easy to throw together, and oh-so refreshing. Sweeten your day with our top-rated watermelonade recipe (and get our best watermelonade serving and storage tips).

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cubed Watermelon
  • Cold Water
  • Lemon Juice
  • Ice Cubes

Recipe

  1. Blend watermelon
  2. Make sugar syrup
  3. Stir in with water and lemon juice
  4. Serve
