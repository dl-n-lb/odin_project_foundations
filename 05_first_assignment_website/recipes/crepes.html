Crepes

Here is a simple but delicious crepe batter which can be made in minutes. It's made from ingredients that everyone has on hand.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter, Melted

Recipe

  1. Whisk flour and eggs, add milk and water gradually
  2. Add butter and salt; beat until smooth
  3. Heat pan over medium heat, pour or scoop onto the griddle and coat evenly
  4. Cook for 2 minutes
